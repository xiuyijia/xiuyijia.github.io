Biography

Currently I am an Assistant Professor in Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NUST). I received my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Technology in Jun. 2005 from Chongqing University, and my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology in Jun. 2011 from Nanjing University. I spent one year (from Sep. 2008 to Sep. 2009) as a visiting graduate research student in Department of Computer Science, University of Regina, Canada. I studied with Professor Yiyu Yao.
